Ingredients:
  • 2 lbs cranberries
  • 2-5 cups sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 apples (peeled / cored / sliced)
  • nutmeg to taste
  • orange zest / juice to taste (1/2 to 1)
  • 1/2 to 1 cup water
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons pectin
  • 6 half pint mason jars
Instructions:
  1. Add cranberries, 2.5 cups of sugar, lemon juice, apples, some nutmeg, and the zest of half an orange to a large pot
  2. Stir together and then place on medium heat
  3. Heat until soft enough to mash, then take of heat and mash with potato masher
  4. Put back on heat and continue simmering. Taste OFTEN, adding more sugar / orange juice / nutmeg as needed
    • NOTE: you have to make the mixture very sweet, as the cranberries will continue to let out their juice throughout the process, especially once you food process them
  5. Simmer for 10-20 minutes, until very soft. Periodically taste and add whatever you want to taste.
  6. Use a food processor (or blender) to mix the jam into desired consistancy
  7. Put back on medium to high heat, add 1/2 to 1 cup water if needed
  8. Add more sugar / spices as needed
  9. Add pectin to mixture, stir well, and bring to a boil
  10. Boil 2 to 5 minutes
    11. Use ladle to scoop jam into mason jars of desired size
  11. Enjoy!
