Mojito

- [ ] 2 oz white rum
- [ ] 1 oz lime juice
- [ ] 2 spoonfuls sugar
- [ ] mint leaves
- [ ] sparkling water

Combine rum, lime juice, and sugar in glass. 

Cover with mint leaves.

Stir throughly, kind of stabbing the mint with the spoon, until sugar is dissolved. 

Fill glass to the top with ice.

Cover with sparkling water.

Stir, garnish with mint leaf, and enjoy!
