Peach “cin” Jam!

Ingredients:
o	3.5 pounds ripe, fresh peaches
o	2 – 4 cups white sugar
o	3 – 6 tablespoons pectin
o	¼ - ½ cup lemon juice (fresh or bottled)
o	Cinnamon to taste
o	6 half pint mason jars

Instructions:
I.	Remove skins and pits from peaches (using whatever method you want) and cut into roughly ½ inch chunks
II.	Add lemon juice to peaches and put them into large pot on medium-low heat 
III.	Cover pot for 3 – 5 minutes, until peaches are soft enough to mash
IV.	Take cover off and heat an additional 3 – 5 minutes, until peaches are soft enough to mash
V.	Take pot off heat and mash with a potato masher 
VI.	Repeat steps until you have desired consistency, using a rotary beater if desired
a.	NOTE: you will be mashing a final time at the end, so it doesn’t have to be perfect
VII.	Mix sugar, cinnamon, and pectin and add to pot, turning heat to medium-low
VIII.	Stir well and cook, stirring constantly, until the flavors have melded (taste constantly)
a.	NOTE: adjust ingredients to taste, start with smallest amount and gradually add until you have the flavor / consistency you want
IX.	Bring to medium-high heat and bring to a boil
X.	Boil 2 – 5 minutes, stirring constantly
XI.	Take pot off heat and mash a final time
XII.	Use ladle to scoop jam into mason jars of desired size, making sure to get an even distribution of fruit chunks
XIII.	Enjoy!

Notes:
	Taste often!! All the ingredients are adjustable, except you have to keep at least a one-half-to-one sugar-to-fruit ratio
	Jam will get firmer after it cools
	Eat within ~6 months~ of cooking, keep refrigerated
