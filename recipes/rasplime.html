Ingredients:
  • 4 cups mashed raspberries
  • Sweetener:
    • 4 cups sugar (traditional)

      • OR

    • 2 cups sugar(low sugar)
    • 4-6 teaspoons pectin

      • OR

    • 1 cup honey (no sugar)
    • 4-6 teaspoons pectin
  • Zest of 2 limes, juice of 3-4 (adjust to taste)
  • 6 half pint mason jars
Instructions:
  1. Use a VERY large pot (jam doubles in size)
  2. Mash berries, zest the limes and squeeze the juice into them
  3. Heat, stirring constantly, on medium heat until they begin to boil
  4. Boil 3 minutes
  5. Pre-mix sugar and pectin (if using)
  6. Add sugar mixture to berries
  7. Stir well and bring heat to medium-high
  8. Bring mixture to a boil, stirring constantly, for ~5 minutes~ (until the jam is slow to fall off the spoon)
  9. Taste (often) and adjust ingredients to taste!
  10. Remove from heat
  11. OPTIONAL: beat with rotary beater until smooth (if you like chunkier jam, omit this step)
  12. Use ladle to scoop jam into mason jars of desired size
  13. OPTIONAL: process cans in water bath to seal (link here)
  14. Enjoy!
Notes:
  • Taste often!! All the ingredients are adjustable, except you have to keep at least a one-half-to-one sugar-to-berry ratio
  • Jam will get firmer after it cools
  • Eat within ~4 months~ of cooking, keep refrigerated
