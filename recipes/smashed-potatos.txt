Smashed Potatos! 
- [ ] Boil small potatoes with LOTS of salt, Rosemary, other spices, and a couple dashes of olive oil (about 15-20 minutes)
- [ ] Mix together olive oil, balsamic, salt, pepper, garlic, etc (enough to cover the taters)
- [ ] Smash taters with a fork / potato smasher onto oil lined pan
- [ ] Put glaze on taters, then put in the oven at 425 for 25-30 minutes
