Instructions:
  1. Whisk egg and soy sauce together in wide bowl
  2. Combine flour, salt, pepper, and Tony's in another wide bowl
  3. Combine panko and optional other spices in another wide bowl
  4. Cut avocado into 1 inch chunks
  5. Dip each avocado chunk into flour mix, then egg, then panko
  6. Place chunks on greased pan, without touching each other
  7. Place on greased pan and bake at 450, 10-20 minutes
