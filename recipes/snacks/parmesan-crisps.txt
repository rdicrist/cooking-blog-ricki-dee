Parmesan Crisps

- [ ] About 2 cups freshly grated parm
- [ ] Rosemary (optional)

- Preheat oven to 400
- Mix up cheese and rosemary then spread onto parchment lined baking sheet, patting down 
- Put in over 3-5 minutes, pat grease off when you take out
- Cool completely! Then break into pieces and enjoy! 

- If not crispy: 
- 
- Heat oven to 200 degrees, 5 minutes, flip, 5 minutes

- If not crispy after cooking AGAIN:
- 
- Heat oven to 200 degrees, 5 minutes, flip, 5 minutes
