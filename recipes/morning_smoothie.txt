Morning Smoothie
- [ ] Frozen raspberries
- [ ] Full fat plain Greek yogurt
- [ ] Almond milk
- [ ] Chocolate protein powder 
- [ ] Coffee 

Shake protein powder into milk. 

Put raspberries followed by yogurt in food processor / blender, and fill about halfway of their height with milk mixture

Blend / pulse until smooth

Stir in desired amount of coffee
